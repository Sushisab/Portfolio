About Me

Hi, I'm Sabrina El-Aouar, a digital artist and web developer. I enjoy creating digital and traditional artwork.

I've always enjoyed both art and technology, which led me to pursue a career as both an artist and a developer. It all began with traditional art in school, where I explored painting, drawing, and sculpture. Over time, my interests shifted toward digital art, where I used my skills in software like Photoshop, Illustrator, and other creative tools.

I have a background in Graphic Design and Web Development, blending my passion for art, design, and technology to create custom artwork.

I am always eager to take on new projects and challenges, whether it's creating a commission for a client or collaborating with other creatives. My goal is to collaborate and work with all kinds of artists and technology enthusiasts, using this opportunity to expand my portfolio!

If you'd like to work with me or have any questions, feel free to reach out. I would love to collaborate!
